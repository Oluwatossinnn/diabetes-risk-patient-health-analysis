# diabetes-risk-patient-health-analysis
Analysis of diabetes prevalence and patient health metrics including BMI, glucose levels, and blood pressure trends

## Key Findings
- **Diabetes Prevalence**: 34.9% of patients (268 out of 768) have diabetes
- **Average BMI**: 32.46 across all patients
- **High-Risk Group**: Obese patients show 46.40% diabetes prevalence
- **Age Factor**: Older patients (45-59) have the highest diabetes rate at 50.41%
- **Glucose Levels**: Diabetic patients average 142 mg/dL vs 111 mg/dL for non-diabetic
- **Insulin Levels**: Diabetic patients show higher average insulin (207) compared to non-diabetic (130)
